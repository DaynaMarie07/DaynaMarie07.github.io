About Me

Dayna Payne

Hello! My name is Dayna Payne, a recent graduate of F.I.R.S.T. institute, in Orlando, Fl. I received a certificate in Graphic Design and Web Development! I continued to crave more knowledge about web development and decided to enroll in UCFs coding bootcamp. By the end of the program I will be a Full Stack Developer. I also, have experience with Adobe products, such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Bridge, etc. I am also a passionate Photographer, I have done weddings, portraits, children, families, events and many more. I have even worked with the talented Dean Cundy, cinematographer, for films such as: 'Jurassic Park', 'Back to the Future' and many more. I incorporate my photography by offering photo content on my clients web sites.
